About Strings

Noodles are made from unleavened (usually wheat) dough which is pulled and pushed into a huge variety of shapes, of which the most common is strings and strips. This name was inspired by the definition of noodles and change in marketing mix of the product would be feasible as new products can be packed and launched with ease because of the relatable name.The tagline of Strings is ‘Any time – Any place, packed with full of taste’. The USP of strings is that it will contain supreme quality of noodle with decent quantity. Skimming pricing strategy will be used with many promotional tools such as advertising, sales promotion (quantity gift, sampling), publicity, and social media as it increases the cost of the product.
